About Us

Servill is a home services platform where odd jobs are no longer odd enough to handle for anyone. We are creating a marketplace platform for the people. A platform for people to meet home service providers that can help with an array of jobs from lawn care to tutoring and everything in between. We are diminishing the challenge in finding quality service providers and going through quotes with providers. Customers can come onto our platform and find a list of providers and select one that meets their needs.

Servill is a unique platform that allows the odd person to freelance their skills and thrive their small business to help customers who need help, we are creating a friendly environment for customers to allow freelancers an opportunity to service others to create a helping community.
Whether you are looking for someone to help your problem or looking for a marketplace to obtain more business, Servill is the marketplace where people help people.
